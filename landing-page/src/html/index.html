What is ERA?

The Evacuee Registration & Assistance (ERA) tool is a new digital platform for Emergency Support Services. ERA is designed to increase the efficiency and speed of service delivery for evacuees across the province.

Find out more >

Be Evacuation Prepared - Register with ERA

Evacuations can be stressful. However, if you take the time now to prepare you can save time and make real situations less stressful.

If you have a BC Services Card and would like to register in preparation for a possible future evacuation, you can now self-register.
