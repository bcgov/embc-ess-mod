Collection Notice

Emergency Management BC (EMBC) will collect your personal information for the purpose of providing you with emergency services in accordance with section 26(c) of the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (FoIPPA). EMBC will also collect the information as per section 26(e) of FoIPPA in order to evaluate the ESS program. Should you have any questions about the collection, use, or disclosure of personal information, please contact the ESS Program Specialist at PO Box 9201, STN PROV GOVT, Victoria, BC, V8W 9J1 or by phone toll free at 1-800-585-9559.
