Security Word

The security word is a vital piece of information to ensure an Emergency Support Services (ESS) Responder can access your ESS File.

If you have been evacuated, please share the answer to this question with any household members that you are registering.

This will enable an Emergency Support Services (ESS) Responder to assist them when you are not able to be present.


Enter a memorable security word that is over 6 characters in length. You may include numbers and letters. The security word is not case sensitive.

For security reasons, your security word should not include your email address.
