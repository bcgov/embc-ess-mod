Preferred Method

Register with BC Services Card

Safe & secure way to complete an online self-registration.
Prepares you & your household for future emergencies.
Access your profile anytime to view supports you have received.
Update your details, as well as view current & past emergency events.
You may be eligible to receive supports via e-Transfer.
Learn more about the BC Services Card app

Register without BC Services Card

If you don't have a BC Services Card, you can still self-register as an evacuee.

You may be eligible to receive supports, but not via e-Transfer.
